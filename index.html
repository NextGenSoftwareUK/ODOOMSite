About ODOOM

Classic Hell, Powered by the Omniverse

ODOOM is a fully modified build of UZDoom — a modern C++ Doom source port — deeply integrated with the OASIS Omniverse via OGEngine and the WEB5 STAR API.

While the classic gameplay loop of ripping and tearing through demonic hordes remains intact, ODOOM extends it into the physical world. Keycards, weapons, and armour are hidden at real GPS coordinates in parks around the world. Players physically walk to those locations to collect items, which then appear in their shared OASIS inventory and unlock in-game.

NFTs are minted automatically for significant kills, creating a permanent on-chain record of your slaughter. Cross-game quests powered by OGEngine let you carry your OASIS avatar and progress across every game in the Omniverse. Karma earned in ODOOM flows through the entire ecosystem.

AR demon combat — coming soon — will bring the invasion to your actual neighbourhood. Real parks, real enemies, augmented reality.

Built on the WEB4 OASIS API with full WEB5 STAR API integration.

Features

Everything Hell Has to Offer

Classic demon-slaying meets decentralised real-world adventure. Eight features that blur the line between gameplay and reality.

🔫
Classic Demon Combat

The full UZDoom experience — tight gunplay, classic Doom enemies, modern source-port stability. All the classic maps, weapons, and the raw satisfaction of pixelated carnage.

📍
Real-World Keycards

Red, blue, and yellow keycards are hidden at real GPS coordinates in parks. Walk there physically to collect them. Your GPS hardware unlocks the corresponding in-game door.

⚔️
IRL Weapons & Armour

Shotguns, rocket launchers, chainsaw shards, and armour pieces are geo-cached in the real world. Find them on your walk home and load up before the next session.

🎒
Shared OASIS Inventory

Every item collected flows into your shared OASIS inventory via the WEB4 OASIS API. Weapons, keycards, and armour persist and transfer across every OASIS game.

🏆
NFT Kill Minting

Legendary kills — boss demons, rare encounters — trigger automatic NFT minting via the STAR API. An immutable on-chain record of your most glorious carnage, tradeable in any Web3 marketplace.

🌐
Cross-Game Quests

Quests powered by OGEngine span ODOOM, OQuake, and every other OASIS game. Objectives, rewards, and progress follow your avatar wherever you play.

🧬
Avatar SSO

One OASIS avatar across all Omniverse games. Karma, achievements, inventory, and identity are persistent. Log in once, play everywhere — ODOOM, OQuake, Our World, and beyond.

📡
AR Demon Combat

Point your phone at a park and watch the invasion begin. Pinky demons behind the bandstand. Cacodemons above the pond. Real-world locations, real-time augmented slaughter — powered by Our World.

Coming Soon
✏️
ODOOM Editor

A fork of the popular Ultimate Doom Editor with a STAR toolbar and menu. Build and edit maps with full placement of Doom and Quake assets — monsters, keys, health, armour, powerups, weapons and items. Deeper OGEngine and STAR API integration is coming soon, enabling quest and mission creation directly within the editor.

📦
Stack Everything

Pick up every item in every map — health packs, armour, ammo, powerups, keycards — even at max capacity. Bank them and choose when to deploy. Finally beat that level you could never get past by stockpiling for days beforehand. And for modders: create brutal new maps designed around stacking — knowing players can prepare across multiple sessions. DOOM becomes an FPS/RPG hybrid and a brand new cross-game modding genre is born.

📤
Send to Avatar / Send to Clan

Farm health packs, powerups, weapons, ammo and keycards and send them directly to clan members fighting fierce battles or tournaments — in ODOOM, OQUAKE or any connected game. Assign a dedicated farmer while your best combat players burn through supplies. Build emergency stockpiles. Coordinate clan logistics across the entire Omniverse. A whole new level of strategy DOOM never had.

🎯
GeoHotSpot Triggers

Embed GeoHotSpot triggers anywhere in your maps — inside quests, as objectives, or standalone. Trigger audio clips, video playback, text overlays, scripting events and NPC spawns at real-world GPS coordinates or specific in-game locations. Mappers can chain multiple triggers together to build entire questlines that only unlock when players are physically near a real-world location, blending level design with reality in ways no Doom map has ever done before.

⚡
Quick Combat Actions

No time to open your inventory mid-battle? Press C to instantly use a health pack or F to slam on armour without breaking combat flow. Bank dozens of supplies from farming sessions and deploy them in a single keypress when it matters most. Every hotkey is fully remappable, so speedrunners and competitive players can bind item usage to whatever layout keeps their hands fastest on the keyboard.

🦸
Karma Powers Your Stats

Do good in real life through Our World — pick up litter, exercise, eat healthy, help your community — and your ODOOM avatar gets measurably stronger. Max health, armour, mana, stamina, ammo and movement speed all increase as your karma grows. At high karma levels, players unlock superpowers — flight, telekinesis, teleportation, super speed, super strength and more — that carry across every OASIS game. The real-life superhero training program is real. You become the Doom Slayer.

⚙️
Fully Configurable

Server operators, tournament organisers and modders control everything via oasisstar.json — configurable locally or on the server. Toggle stacking per item type, set custom max health and armour caps, choose which items and monster kills mint as NFTs (configurable per monster type), enable auto-use on pickup, set the NFT provider, and much more. In future, all settings will also be configurable directly through OPORTAL and STARNET.

📶
Offline Play & Sync

Play the full ODOOM experience without an internet connection. Progress, inventory, and quest data sync automatically the moment you reconnect — ideal for poor or intermittent connectivity anywhere in the world. Built on the OASIS Hyperdrive's offline-first architecture, so your karma, farmed supplies and quest state are never at risk, even on a multi-day trip off-grid.

🐾
Pet Companions

Deploy collected powerups and special items as pet companions that fight alongside you. Assign pets to clan members mid-battle — whether they're in ODOOM, OQUAKE or any other connected Omniverse game.

Coming Soon
🌐
OASIS Omniverse HUB

A persistent inter-dimensional hub letting you teleport between any map in any connected game instantly — carrying your full inventory, karma and quest state with you. One portal. Every world.

🖥️
Shared HUD

A unified heads-up display across every OASIS game — live inventory counts, active quests, friend locations, messages and avatar stats visible at a glance, whether you're in ODOOM, OQUAKE or Our World.

🏴
Clan Management

Form and manage OASIS clans with full role assignment — farmers, combat specialists, scouts. Track shared stockpiles, coordinate supply drops mid-battle, build emergency reserves, and wage clan-vs-clan wars across the entire Omniverse.
